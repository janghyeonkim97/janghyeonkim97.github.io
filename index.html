Janghyeon Kim

Welcome! I'm a robotics engineer at the Human Assistive Neurorobotics (HAN) Lab at Yonsei University. I received an M.S. degree in Computer Science from Yonsei University, supervised by Prof. Han Ul Yoon. My research interests include robot learning, human-robot interaction, and AI for robotics. In particular, I focus on developing intelligent robotic systems that can learn from human demonstrations and adapt to dynamic environments.


Janghyeon Kim is a robotic engineer specializing in autonomous systems and machine learning for robotics. He has worked on various projects involving robot perception, manipulation, and control. His work focuses on building intelligent systems that can operate effectively in complex environments.
